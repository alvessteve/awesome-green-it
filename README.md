# awesome-green-it

An awesome list of sites/books/tools/podcasts about green IT and sustainable software engineering. Because ecology matters!

## Context & Research

The necessity of changing our habits:
- [World Scientists' Warning to Humanity (2017)](https://academic.oup.com/bioscience/article-abstract/doi/10.1093/biosci/bix125/4605229?redirectedFrom=fulltext)
- [The Shift Project - Lean ICT: Towards Digital Sobriety](https://theshiftproject.org/en/publications/lean-ict/) - Research on ICT energy consumption and digital sobriety (2018-2024)
- [The Shift Project - Deploying Digital Sobriety](https://theshiftproject.org/en/publications/deploying-digital-sobriety/) - Latest 2024 report on lean networks for resilient connected uses

## Books & Publications

### Recent Publications (2024-2025)
- [Digital Sustainability: Leveraging Digital Technology to Combat Climate Change](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-031-61749-2) - Open access book (Springer, 2024) on digital technology for climate action
- [EcoDesign for Sustainable Products, Services and Social Systems](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-981-99-3818-6) - Comprehensive guide on circular economy and digital technologies (Springer, 2024)
- [Eco-Conscious Software Development: A Comprehensive Guide for Sustainable Practices](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-031-63543-4_2) - Latest practices in sustainable software (Springer, 2024)

## Standards & Specifications

- [Software Carbon Intensity (SCI) Specification](https://sci.greensoftware.foundation/) - ISO/IEC 21031:2024 standard for measuring software carbon intensity
- [Green Software Practitioner Certification (LFC131)](https://learn.greensoftware.foundation/) - Free training and certification from Linux Foundation (130,000+ developers certified)
- [Greenhouse Gas Protocol](https://ghgprotocol.org/) - Corporate standard for measuring carbon emissions

## Tools & Frameworks

### Carbon Measurement
- [Website Carbon Calculator](https://www.websitecarbon.com/) - Calculate your website's CO2 emissions
- [Digital Carbon Online](https://www.digitalcarbon.online/) - Website carbon footprint calculator
- [Cloud Carbon Footprint](https://www.cloudcarbonfootprint.org/) - Open source tool to measure cloud carbon emissions
- [Green Metrics Tool](https://github.com/Green-Software-Foundation/awesome-green-software) - Impact Framework for measuring environmental impacts

### Cloud Provider Tools (2024-2025)
- [Google Cloud Carbon Footprint](https://cloud.google.com/carbon-footprint) - Carbon Sense suite with BigQuery integration
- [Microsoft Azure Carbon Optimization](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/solutions/sustainability/) - Emissions Impact Dashboard with actionable optimization guidance
- [AWS Customer Carbon Footprint Tool](https://aws.amazon.com/sustainability/) - Track and forecast AWS usage emissions

### Energy Profiling
- [Green Software Foundation Impact Framework](https://github.com/Green-Software-Foundation/if) - Framework for calculating environmental impacts
- [Scaphandre](https://github.com/hubblo-org/scaphandre) - Energy consumption metrology agent
- [CodeCarbon](https://codecarbon.io/) - Track and reduce CO2 emissions from your code
